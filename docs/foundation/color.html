Usage Guidelines

Consistent Palette

Always use colors defined in the library to ensure consistency across the application. Avoid using custom colors unless necessary and approved by the design system.

Purpose-Driven Colors

Apply colors based on their intended purpose. For instance, use primary colors for buttons and links, secondary colors for accents, and background colors for general layout sections.

Contrast & Accessibility

Ensure that text and important elements have enough contrast against their background. This guarantees readability and accessibility for all users, especially those with visual impairments.

Limit Color Usage

Avoid excessive use of bright or bold colors, particularly in the body text or background areas. Stick to a clean, minimalist design by using a maximum of 3 to 4 main colors in a given layout or component.
